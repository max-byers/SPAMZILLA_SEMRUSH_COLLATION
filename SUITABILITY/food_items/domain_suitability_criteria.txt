DOMAIN SUITABILITY CRITERIA
==========================

1. FOOD-RELATED TERMS
---------------------
Domains containing terms related to:
- Food and beverages (food, drink, eat, meal)
- Dining establishments (restaurant, cafe, bistro, diner)
- Food preparation (cooking, baking, grilling)
- Food types (meat, seafood, vegetables, fruits)
- Food industry (catering, delivery, dining)
- Kitchen equipment (stove, oven, utensils)
- Food-related businesses (bakery, butcher, grocery)
- Beverages (coffee, tea, wine, beer)
- Meals and courses (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
- Food quality terms (organic, gourmet, vegan)

2. NON-LITERATE WORDS
---------------------
- Domains with random letter combinations that don't form recognizable words
- Exception: If non-literate portion is 4 letters or less
- Exception: If combines literate words with short non-literate segments

3. FULL NAMES
-------------
- Domains containing clear first+last name combinations
- Single names by themselves are acceptable

4. LOCATIONS
------------
- Domains containing geographic locations (cities, countries, regions)
- Exception: "Australia" and "Melbourne" are allowed locations
- Location acronyms (e.g., "ny" for New York, "la" for Los Angeles)

5. BUSINESS INDICATORS (these make domains SUITABLE)
---------------------------------------------------
- Restaurants: cafe, restaurant, bistro, diner, eatery, grill
- Services: services, solutions, consulting, agency
- Retail: shop, store, market, boutique, mart
- Professional: studio, agency, company, firm, group, inc, llc
- Specific Industries: brewery, winery, bakery, tech, media

6. CONCEPTUAL VS. SPECIFIC
-------------------------
- Prefer broad concepts that allow discussing various topics
- Less desirable are very specific items limiting content scope

7. BRAND/TRADEMARK CONCERNS
---------------------------
- Domains that clearly reference major brands or trademarks
- Examples: microsoft, apple, google

Note: This criteria list is regularly updated as new patterns are identified.
