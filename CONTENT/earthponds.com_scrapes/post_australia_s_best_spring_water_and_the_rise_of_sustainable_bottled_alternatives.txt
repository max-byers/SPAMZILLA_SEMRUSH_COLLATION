Pure by Nature – The Essence of Spring Water, Australia’s Best Water
Spring water comes straight from natural springs and is beloved for its purity and rich mineral content. Unlike tap water, which may undergo extensive treatment, spring water is naturally filtered through layers of rock, adding minerals such as calcium, magnesium, and potassium. These minerals not only enhance the flavor but are also believed to improve hydration and promote health.
For example, the pH level of many Australian spring waters ranges from 7.0 to 8.5, which is considered more alkaline than regular tap water. This alkalinity, combined with essential minerals, makes spring water a refreshing choice that many Australians are now opting for. In fact, a recent survey showed that over 60% of Australians prefer drinking spring water due to its taste and believed health benefits.

Australian vs. Imported Water Brands
When it comes to bottled water, Australians are spoiled for choice. The discussion around choosing homegrown brands versus imported options has become lively. Local brands like Mount Franklin and Pump are recognized not only for their quality but also for their commitment to local sourcing. Mount Franklin, for instance, sources its water from pristine springs in New South Wales, contributing to local employment and economic support.
On the flip side, imported waters such as Evian and San Pellegrino bring unique flavors that appeal to adventurous consumers. However, opting for Australian brands often means supporting better environmental practices. Shipping water across continents increases carbon emissions significantly, while local brands keep their environmental footprint minimal.
In fact, studies reveal that local sourcing can reduce transportation-related carbon emissions by up to 80%. By choosing Australian spring water, consumers can directly contribute to a healthier planet while enjoying superior quality.
The Sustainability Packaging Trend
The growing awareness of environmental issues has put sustainable packaging in the spotlight. Brands like Aquaworks are making significant strides in this area, utilizing materials such as BPA-free plastic, aluminum, and glass that align with eco-friendly principles.
Aquaworks stands out by emphasizing sustainability without sacrificing quality. Their BPA-free bottles provide peace of mind for health-conscious individuals, ensuring no harmful chemicals leach into their water.

Glass bottles, although a premium option, provide an elegant choice that resonates with consumers looking for a unique drinking experience.
Sparkling Water vs. Still Water: A Taste of Preference
In Australia, sparkling and still water have their loyal followers. Sparkling water has gained traction as a flavorful alternative to sugary soft drinks. The bubbles add a festive touch to any occasion, whether it’s a barbecue or a cozy dinner at home.
Conversely, still water remains a staple in many Australian households. It is often seen as the healthier daily choice due to its easy digestion. In fact, according to health reports, 75% of Australians prefer still water for their regular hydration needs.
Aquaworks is adept at providing options for both tastes, ensuring everyone can find a suitable product that meets their hydration preferences.
