A COMMITMENT TO SUSTAINABILITY
At Gilderoy Springs, we cherish the natural beauty and cleanliness of the environment that our water comes from. As an organisation, we are committed to ensuring that we do everything that we can to minimise our impact on the environment, not only around our home in Victoria, but also around the world where our product is consumed.
When sourcing materials and partners for our business, we invest heavily in research and due diligence to ensure our ethical standards and commitment to sustainability are enhanced.
Our flexible packaging uses less resources to manufacture and is 90% more sustainable than glass due to less weight in transportation. Aseptic packaging uses 70% less energy from the factory to transport with no refrigeration required.
